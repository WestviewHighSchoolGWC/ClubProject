College and Career Pathways

The career pathway program gives you information on what courses to take in order to gain skills related to your career area of interest. This will help prepare you for study at colleges, universities, technical institution, or other post-high school opportunities. To complete a pathway and earn a cord at graduation, you must: - Explore pathways of interests during your 9th grade year. - Check in the Pathway Lead for your chosen pathway and forecast for the required classes. - Complete a Career Related Leading Experience (CRLE) related to pathway. - Complete a pathway project. Check with project lead for requirements!

Colleges Visiting Westview

Students: The college rep schedules are posted on Canvas. Students: please sign up in the CCC prior to visit, and get a pass for 7/8 period. Presentations are open to all students.

Career Education

9th Grade: - Students complete a CRLE - CIS Work - Four Year Plan- with post secondary goals - Set goal for the School Year - Interest Profiler - Research Post Secondary Options

10th Grade: - Students complete a CRLE - CIS Work - Explore three occupations - Set school year goal - Complete a job application - Update four year plan-with post secondary goals

11th Grade: - Students complete a CRLE - CIS Work - Set school year goal - Complete a job application - Update four year plan-with post secondary goals - Resume - Mock Interview

12th Grade: - Students complete a CRLE - Complete Senior Project - CIS Work - Set after high school goals - Update 4 year plan to include 2 years after high school
